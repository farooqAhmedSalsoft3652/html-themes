The Idea

At follow My Food Trucks, we are foodies that are also food truck fanatics.

We love the convenience pf eating on the go. A delicious yet fast and economical dining experience that can only be enjoyed at a food truck.

While traveling or relocating to new city, we always had this one issue. We had no idea about the lay of the land. While Google Maps is excellent for locating eateries, it proved ineffective for monitoring of food truck possibilities.

As a Result, FMFT was born
